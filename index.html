HOW TO BE A PART?

We are a home of diversity, difference and challenge


No matter who you are, what you do or where you live, you are welcome in QTN

When signing up an account on our website, you can:

  • Receive lasted news
  • Find online free courses
  • Update projects, events
  • Follow successful QTNers
  • Contact with other users

You can register to be a QTNer, then, if accepted, joining a suitable chapter and:

  • Explore profiles of QTN members
  • Earn badges for your online CVs
  • Launch your own events and projects
  • Be followed by other users

By registering to open a new Chapter, you can

  • Call for attendance from users
  • Implement QTN Global’s activities
  • Create Chapter’s own activities
  • Receive supports from other QTN Global
